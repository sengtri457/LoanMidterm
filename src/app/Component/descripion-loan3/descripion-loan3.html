Understanding Your Loan

How It Works

Our loan calculator uses a unique 3-month payment structure where you pay interest monthly but make principal payments every third month. This approach helps maintain steady cash flow while systematically reducing your debt.

Interest Calculation

Interest is calculated on the remaining principal balance each month. As you pay down the principal every third month, your interest payments decrease over time, saving you money.

Benefits

This payment structure offers better cash flow management, predictable payment schedules, and the flexibility to plan your finances around larger principal payments.
