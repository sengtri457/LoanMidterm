How Our Loan System Works

Transparent, Simple, and Easy to Understand

Loan Breakdown

Loan Amount

This is the total amount you want to borrow. The system allows any positive number.

Interest Rate

A fixed monthly percentage is added to your loan. For example, 2% monthly interest.

Duration

You can choose how many months to pay it back. The system splits payments monthly.

Example Calculation

Loan: $2,000

Interest Rate: 2% per month

Duration: 10 months

Monthly Principal: $200

Monthly Interest: $40

Monthly Payment: $240

Total Interest Paid: $400

Grand Total: $2,400
